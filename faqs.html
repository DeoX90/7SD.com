What services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive mobile detailing services, including exterior detailing, interior detailing, scratch removal. All offered within our packages: Interior, Deluxe, Premium, and Exterior.

How long does the detailing process usually take?

The duration depends on the package you choose. Our Express service takes approximately 1 hour and 30 minutes, while Interior, Deluxe and Premium packages may take longer for a more comprehensive clean.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve locations within a 30 miles radius from Prior Lake. For specific locations, please check our website or contact us for more details.

Can I schedule recurring detailing services?

Yes, we offer recurring detailing services at discounted rates. Contact us for more information on our customizable maintenance plans.

Are your products safe for my vehicle's paint and interior surfaces?

Yes, we use only premium, industry-approved products that are safe for your vehicle's paint and interior surfaces. Our detailers are trained to use these products effectively.

What forms of payment do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and cash payments. You can also prepay through our website when booking your appointment.

What is 7 Step Detailers?

7 Step Detailers is a mobile detailing company that provides top-notch car care services at your doorstep. Our team of skilled professionals offers exterior and interior detailing, scratch removal, odor elimination, and deluxe packages.

How does mobile detailing work?

Our mobile detailing service allows us to come to your location, whether it's your home or office. Our fully equipped team brings all the necessary tools and products to provide a thorough detailing service at your convenience.

How can I book an appointment?

You can easily book an appointment through our website or by calling our customer service. Select your preferred package, date, and time, and we'll take care of the rest.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule my appointment?

We understand that plans can change. Please notify us at least 24 hours in advance, and we'll be happy to reschedule your appointment.

How can I contact 7 Step Detailers for further inquiries?

You can reach us through our website's contact form or by calling our customer service hotline. We're here to assist you with any questions or concerns.
